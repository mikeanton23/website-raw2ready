BacDive Explorer

Microbial knowledge retrieval using the BacDive bacterial metadatabase.

Overview

The BacDive Explorer integrates microbial knowledge into Raw2Ready by connecting fermentation experiments with the Bacterial Diversity Metadatabase (BacDive).

Instead of relying only on experimental measurements, Raw2Ready enriches datasets with biological information including strain characteristics, cultivation conditions, physiology, taxonomy and environmental preferences.

This additional context allows AI agents to interpret fermentation behaviour using both experimental evidence and established microbiological knowledge.

Objectives

  • Retrieve microbial information.
  • Support strain identification.
  • Provide cultivation conditions.
  • Retrieve physiological properties.
  • Improve biological interpretation.
  • Support AI-assisted reasoning.
  • Integrate external biological knowledge.

Why BacDive?

Experimental datasets describe what happened during a fermentation process, while BacDive explains why those observations may have occurred.

Combining numerical measurements with microbial knowledge creates a richer understanding of experimental behaviour.

Raw2Ready combines experimental evidence with biological knowledge to support evidence-based interpretation.

Best Practices

  • Use complete scientific names whenever possible.
  • Verify microorganism strain identifiers.
  • Combine BacDive information with metadata.
  • Compare retrieved growth conditions with experimental observations.
  • Use literature evidence to validate biological conclusions.
  • Review retrieved information before exporting.

Troubleshooting

Problem Possible Cause Recommended Solution
No BacDive record found Unknown strain Verify the scientific name.
Incomplete information Limited BacDive record Supplement with literature evidence.
Connection failed Internet unavailable Check network connectivity.
Incorrect organism Misspelled species name Validate taxonomy before querying.
Missing AI interpretation Metadata unavailable Complete the Metadata Module first.

Summary

The BacDive Explorer enriches fermentation experiments with curated microbiological knowledge retrieved from the BacDive bacterial metadatabase.

Combined with experimental measurements, metadata and scientific literature, BacDive enables biologically informed reasoning that significantly improves the interpretability of fermentation experiments.

Biological knowledge transforms experimental measurements into scientifically meaningful interpretations.
